Who I Am

About Me

I'm a Senior Lead Technical Writer with over 10 years of experience building scalable documentation systems for SaaS, enterprise software, and AI-native products.

I turn complex products into documentation people can actually use — from information architecture and content strategy through to in-app UX copy, API guides, release notes, and structured onboarding content.

Currently I own end-to-end documentation for Document360 and Floik at Kovai.co, leading a team of technical writers and setting standards across the full documentation lifecycle.

My engineering background gives me an uncommon ability to read source code, parse product specs, and ask the right questions of engineering teams. Previously an assistant professor and published researcher (8 papers, 2 patents), I bring the ability to absorb deeply technical material and make it clear, accurate, and findable.

My Documentation Philosophy

I follow the Diátaxis framework — structuring content into tutorials, how-to guides, explanations, and references — and combine it with analytics (GA4, Hotjar, Clarity) to let actual user behaviour guide what gets written, updated, or retired. Documentation should be discoverable, accurate, and alive.
